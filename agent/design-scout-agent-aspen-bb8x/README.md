# Design Scout Agent

![price: free](https://img.shields.io/badge/price-free-brightgreen) ![forkable](https://img.shields.io/badge/forkable-yes-blue) ![license: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-informational)

A specialized job scouting agent that researches in-house illustrative graphic design opportunities in Singapore paying 3,000+ SGD. It identifies target companies (not agencies) across consumer brands, retail, property, hospitality, tech, and cultural sectors, then surfaces the HR contacts and hiring managers who can open doors. Built for a graduating NUS Industrial Design student with freelance experience including a large mural project and 6-7k SGD project budgets.

## How it works
1. Researches companies with in-house creative/design teams
2. Finds active job openings or target roles paying 3k+ SGD
3. Identifies HR and hiring manager contacts
4. Assesses fit based on Industrial Design background and freelance experience
5. Provides actionable next steps for each opportunity

## Target industries
Consumer brands & FMCG, retail & apparel, property & real estate, hospitality & F&B, tech companies, cultural institutions, education & publishing, healthcare & pharmaceuticals — any company with an in-house design team that is NOT a design/advertising agency.
